Privacy Policy for Draema App

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Draema mobile application (the "App") that generates movie-like comics from text using generative AI.

Information We Collect

When you use the Draema App, we collect information you provide directly to us, such as the text input you enter into the application. This text may include personal information depending on what you choose to input. We do not collect any other personal information automatically.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to generate movie-like comics based on the text input you provide. The text you enter is processed by our generative AI algorithms to create visual content within the app. We do not use your information for any other purpose without your consent.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or to comply with a legal obligation.

Data Retention

We retain the text input you provide within the app for the sole purpose of generating movie-like comics. We do not store this information on our servers, and it is only processed locally within the app.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
